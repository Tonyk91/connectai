# How to make your first international call

Making an international call with ConnectTel takes less than a minute once your account is verified.

1. Open the ConnectTel app and tap **Call**.
2. Select the destination country from the list, or type the full number including the country code (for example, +234 for Nigeria or +63 for the Philippines).
3. Tap the green call button. The app routes your call over the local access number so you are charged local rates, not roaming rates.
4. If this is your first call, you may be asked to confirm your phone number with a one-time code sent by SMS.

You do not need the person you are calling to have the app installed. They receive a normal incoming call on any phone, mobile or landline.

**Saving contacts:** Tap the star icon next to any number to save it as a favourite so it appears at the top of your Call screen.

**Still not working?** If the call does not connect, check that you have either an active data connection or remaining call credit, and that the destination number includes the correct country code. Calls to premium or satellite numbers are not supported.
