# App freezes, crashes, or won't open

If the ConnectTel app freezes, closes unexpectedly, or will not open, these steps resolve almost all cases.

**Basic steps in order:**
1. Fully close the app (swipe it away from recent apps) and reopen it.
2. Restart your phone — this clears temporary memory issues that cause freezes.
3. Check for an app update in the App Store or Google Play. Crashes are often fixed in the latest version.
4. Make sure your phone's operating system is up to date, as very old OS versions are not supported.

**If it still crashes:**
- Clear the app's cache (Android: **Settings → Apps → ConnectTel → Storage → Clear cache**). This does not delete your account.
- As a last resort, uninstall and reinstall. Your account and history are stored in the cloud and restored when you log back in.

**Crashing only during calls:** This usually points to a permissions issue. Confirm microphone permission is granted under your phone's settings.

**Still crashing?** If reinstalling does not help, contact support and tell us your phone model and OS version, and whether the crash happens at a specific screen, so we can reproduce and fix it.
