# Top-up paid but not received by the recipient

Most top-ups arrive within seconds, but occasionally credit is delayed or appears to be missing.

**First, confirm it was actually sent:** Open **Account → History** and check the status of the top-up. A status of *Delivered* means the operator confirmed the credit was applied.

**If status is Delivered but the recipient sees no credit:**
- Ask the recipient to check their balance again after restarting their phone. Some operators apply credit to a promotional or bonus balance that is shown separately from the main balance.
- Confirm you sent to the exact number the recipient uses, with no digits transposed.
- Some operators apply data top-ups as a data bundle rather than monetary credit, so the main balance will not change.

**If status is Pending for more than 30 minutes:** This is rare and usually indicates an operator-side delay. The credit will still be delivered; you do not need to send it again.

**If status is Failed:** No credit was sent and you were not charged. You can safely retry.

**Still missing?** If the status is Delivered, the number is correct, and the recipient genuinely received nothing after 24 hours, contact support with the transaction ID from the history screen so we can open an operator trace.
