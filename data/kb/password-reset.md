# Resetting your password or PIN

ConnectTel logs you in with a one-time SMS code, but you can also set an optional password or a security PIN for extra protection on sensitive actions.

**Reset your password:**
1. Go to **Account → Security → Password**.
2. Tap **Reset password**. We send a confirmation code to your registered number.
3. Enter the code and choose a new password of at least 8 characters.

**Forgot your PIN:** The PIN protects actions like changing your payment method. If you forget it, tap **Forgot PIN** on the PIN prompt, verify with the SMS code, and set a new PIN.

**You don't need the old one:** Because resets are confirmed by the SMS code sent to your verified number, you can reset a password or PIN even if you have forgotten the current value.

**Keep your number current:** Since recovery depends on your registered phone number, update it under **Account → Security** before you lose access to an old number.

**Still can't reset?** If you no longer receive SMS on the registered number, password reset will not complete. Contact support to verify your identity and recover the account.
