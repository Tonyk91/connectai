# Trouble logging in to your account

If you cannot log in, the cause is usually the verification step, a new device, or the wrong account identifier.

**Logging in on a new device:** ConnectTel logs you in with your phone number plus a one-time code sent by SMS. Open the app, tap **Log in**, enter your number, and type the code. You do not need to remember a username.

**Code not arriving:**
- Confirm the number is entered with the correct country code.
- Wait 60 seconds and tap **Resend**, or choose **Call me instead** for a voice code.
- Check that your phone has signal and can receive SMS from short codes.

**"Account not found":** You may be entering a different number than the one you registered. Try other numbers you own. Each account is tied to exactly one phone number.

**Locked out after too many attempts:** For your security, repeated failed codes temporarily lock login for 30 minutes. Wait and try again, or contact support to verify your identity another way.

**Changed phone number?** If you no longer have access to the registered number, you cannot receive the code. Contact support with proof of the old number so we can move the account to your new number.

**Still locked out?** Reinstalling the app does not delete your account or data, so it is safe to try as a last step.
