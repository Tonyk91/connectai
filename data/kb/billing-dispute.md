# Disputing a charge you do not recognise

If you see a ConnectTel charge you do not recognise, most cases have a simple explanation before it needs to be disputed.

**Check these first:**
- **Subscriptions and bundles** renew automatically. Review **Account → Subscriptions** to see active renewals.
- **Auto-recharge** tops up your balance automatically when it runs low. Check **Account → Auto-recharge**.
- A *pending* amount that does not match a purchase is usually a temporary card authorisation that will be released, not a charge.
- Charges may appear under our payment processor's name on your statement rather than "ConnectTel".

**If it is still unexplained:**
1. Open **Account → History** and match each charge to a call, top-up, or subscription.
2. Note the date, amount, and any transaction ID for charges you cannot account for.
3. Contact support with those details. We can identify the charge and, if it was made in error, reverse it.

**Unauthorised use:** If you believe someone else used your account, change your password immediately under **Account → Security** and contact support so we can secure the account and review recent activity.

**Card chargebacks:** Filing a chargeback with your bank before contacting us can lock the account during the investigation, so reaching out to support first is usually faster.
