# Data-free calling and how it works

ConnectTel offers data-free calling so you can make international calls even when you have no mobile data or Wi-Fi.

**How it works:** Instead of routing the call over the internet, the app dials a local access number in your country. From your phone's point of view it is an ordinary local call, so it uses your carrier's normal voice minutes, not data. ConnectTel then connects that local leg to the international destination.

**When to use it:** Data-free calling is ideal when you are travelling, on a weak network, or want to avoid using your data allowance. You can set it as the default under **Settings → Calling mode → Data-free**.

**What it costs:** You pay the ConnectTel international per-minute rate as usual. In addition, the local access leg uses your mobile plan's domestic minutes. If you have unlimited local minutes, the access leg is effectively free.

**Requirements:** Data-free calling needs the ability to place a normal voice call on your SIM. It is not available on data-only SIMs or Wi-Fi-only devices such as tablets without a phone plan.

**Still not connecting?** Make sure your phone is not in airplane mode and that you can place a normal local call. If your carrier blocks the access number, contact support and we will provide an alternative access number for your region.
