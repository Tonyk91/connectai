# Creating and verifying your account

You need a verified ConnectTel account before you can call or send top-ups.

**Create an account:**
1. Download the ConnectTel app and tap **Sign up**.
2. Enter your phone number. This number identifies your account and is where verification codes are sent.
3. Enter the 6-digit code we send by SMS to verify the number.
4. Add your name and an email address for receipts and account recovery.

**Why verification matters:** Verifying your phone number protects your account and is required by regulations for international communication services. Unverified accounts cannot place calls.

**Didn't get the code?** Wait 60 seconds and tap **Resend**. Make sure the number includes the correct country code and can receive SMS. If you are on a landline or a number that cannot receive SMS, choose **Call me instead** to receive the code by voice.

**One account per number:** A phone number can only be tied to one active account. If you see "number already in use", you likely already have an account — use **Log in** and recover access instead of signing up again.

**Still stuck?** If verification repeatedly fails, contact support with the phone number you are trying to register so we can check whether SMS delivery to your carrier is blocked.
