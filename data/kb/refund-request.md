# How to request a refund

If a charge is eligible under the refund policy, requesting a refund takes a few minutes.

**Steps:**
1. Open **Account → History** and find the transaction.
2. Tap the transaction, then tap **Request refund**. If the option is not shown, the item is not eligible (for example, a delivered top-up).
3. Choose a reason and submit. You will receive a confirmation with a reference number.

**What happens next:** Most eligible refunds for failed top-ups and duplicate charges are approved automatically and processed the same day. Refunds of unused call credit are reviewed and usually approved within 1–2 business days.

**Refund timing:** Once approved, the money returns to your original payment method. Card refunds take 3–10 business days to appear on your statement; wallet refunds are usually faster.

**Refund to a different method:** Refunds can only go back to the method originally charged. If that card is closed, contact support and we will arrange an alternative.

**Top-up sent to the wrong number:** Because a delivered top-up cannot be reversed, we cannot refund credit that reached a valid but unintended number. Always double-check the recipient number before confirming.

**Still waiting?** If an approved refund has not appeared after 10 business days, contact support with your refund reference number so we can trace it with the payment processor.
