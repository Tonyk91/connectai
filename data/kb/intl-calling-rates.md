# Understanding international calling rates

ConnectTel charges per-minute rates that depend on the destination country and whether you are calling a mobile or a landline.

**Where to see rates:** Open the app, go to **Call → Rates**, and search for a country. The rate shown is the price per minute in your account currency. Rates to landlines are usually cheaper than to mobiles.

**How billing works:** Calls are billed per minute, rounded up to the next full minute. A 2 minute 10 second call is billed as 3 minutes. There is no connection fee.

**Rate changes:** Carrier rates can change from month to month. We update published rates at least 48 hours before they take effect, and the new rate is always the one shown in the app at the moment you place the call.

**Subscriptions vs pay-as-you-go:** If you call one country often, a monthly country bundle can lower your per-minute cost. Bundles renew automatically and can be cancelled any time from **Account → Subscriptions**.

**Still have questions?** Rates never include taxes that may be applied by your card issuer for foreign-currency transactions. Contact support if a charged rate does not match the rate shown in the app at call time.
