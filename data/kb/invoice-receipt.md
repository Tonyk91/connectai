# Finding invoices and receipts

Every payment on ConnectTel — calls, top-ups, subscriptions, and recharges — generates a receipt you can view, download, or share.

**Find a single receipt:**
1. Open **Account → History**.
2. Tap the transaction you want.
3. Tap **Receipt** to view it, then use the share icon to email or save it as a PDF.

**Monthly invoice:** If you need a consolidated invoice covering a whole month, go to **Account → Billing → Invoices** and select the month. This is useful for expense reports.

**Add billing details:** To show a company name, address, or tax/VAT number on your invoices, enter them under **Account → Billing → Billing details** before generating the invoice. Existing receipts cannot be edited after they are issued, so add details first.

**Email receipts automatically:** Enable **Email me receipts** under billing settings to receive a receipt by email after every payment.

**Still can't find it?** Receipts are available for the life of the account. If a transaction is missing from your history, make sure you are signed in to the correct account, since top-ups can be made from more than one account tied to the same phone number.
