# ConnectTel refund policy

This policy explains what is refundable and what is not.

**Refundable:**
- **Unused call credit** purchased within the last 12 months can be refunded to the original payment method, minus any credit already spent.
- **Failed top-ups** where the credit was never delivered are refunded automatically; you do not need to request these.
- **Duplicate charges** caused by a technical error are always refunded in full.
- A **subscription** cancelled within 14 days of its first purchase, if unused, is eligible for a refund.

**Not refundable:**
- **Delivered top-ups.** Once a top-up is confirmed delivered to the recipient's operator, it cannot be reversed, because the operator has already applied the credit.
- **Call credit already consumed** by completed calls.
- **Subscription periods already used.**

**How refunds are paid:** Refunds go back to the original payment method. Card refunds typically take 3–10 business days to appear, depending on your bank.

**Currency:** Refunds are issued in your account currency. If your card is in a different currency, the amount you receive may differ slightly from the original charge due to exchange-rate movement.

**Questions about a specific charge?** See "How to request a refund" for the step-by-step process, or contact support with the transaction ID.
