# Fixing poor call quality or dropped audio

Choppy audio, echo, or one-way sound on international calls is almost always caused by the network connection rather than the destination line.

**Quick checks:**
- Move to an area with a stronger Wi-Fi or mobile signal. Audio calls need only a stable connection, not a fast one.
- If you are on Wi-Fi and quality is poor, try switching to mobile data, or vice versa.
- Close other apps that are streaming video or downloading in the background.

**Echo:** Echo is usually caused by the other person's speakerphone. Ask them to use a headset or lower their speaker volume.

**One-way audio:** If you can hear them but they cannot hear you, check that the app has microphone permission under your phone's settings, and that you are not muted.

**Call mode:** Under **Settings → Call quality**, you can switch between *High quality* and *Low bandwidth*. Low bandwidth uses less data and connects better on weak networks.

**Still poor?** If quality is bad on every call regardless of network, fully close and reopen the app, then place a test call. Persistent problems on a specific destination may be a temporary carrier issue that resolves within a few hours.
