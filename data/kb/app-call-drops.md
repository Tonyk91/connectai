# Calls drop or fail to connect from the app

When calls end early or never connect, the problem is usually the network path or app permissions rather than your credit.

**Calls won't connect at all:**
- Confirm you have either call credit or an active data connection.
- Check the destination number includes the correct country code.
- Verify the app has permission to make calls and use the microphone.

**Calls drop after a few seconds or minutes:**
- An unstable Wi-Fi or mobile signal is the most common cause. Move to better coverage or switch between Wi-Fi and mobile data.
- Battery-saver or "doze" modes can suspend the app in the background. Exclude ConnectTel from battery optimisation in your phone settings so calls are not cut when the screen locks.
- If you are on data-free calling, a dropped call may mean your carrier ended the local access leg; try switching to internet calling under **Settings → Calling mode**.

**One specific destination always drops:** This can be a temporary carrier issue on that route, which typically clears within a few hours.

**Still dropping?** If calls drop on every network and every destination, reinstall the app, then contact support with the time of a recent dropped call so we can check the routing logs.
