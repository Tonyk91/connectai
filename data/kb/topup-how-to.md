# How to send a mobile top-up

A mobile top-up adds airtime or data credit to any supported prepaid phone number, including numbers abroad for friends and family.

1. In the app, tap **Top-up**.
2. Enter or select the recipient's phone number. Make sure it includes the correct country code.
3. The app detects the mobile operator automatically. If detection is wrong, tap **Change operator** and pick the correct one.
4. Choose an amount. Some operators offer fixed denominations only; others allow a custom amount within a range.
5. Confirm and pay. The credit is usually delivered to the recipient within seconds, and you both receive a confirmation.

**Sending to yourself:** You can top up your own number the same way — just enter your own phone number as the recipient.

**Receipts:** Every top-up generates a receipt under **Account → History**. Tap any entry to view or share the receipt.

**Still need help?** If you are unsure which operator a number belongs to, the recipient can check by dialling their operator's balance code. Sending to the wrong operator is the most common cause of a top-up not arriving.
