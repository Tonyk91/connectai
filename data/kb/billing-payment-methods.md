# Adding and managing payment methods

ConnectTel supports credit and debit cards, and in many regions also digital wallets such as Apple Pay and Google Pay.

**Add a payment method:**
1. Go to **Account → Payment methods**.
2. Tap **Add method** and enter your card details or link a wallet.
3. A small temporary authorisation may be placed to verify the card; it is released automatically.

**Set a default:** Tap any saved method and choose **Set as default**. The default is used for subscriptions and auto-recharge.

**Remove a method:** Tap the method and choose **Remove**. You cannot remove the only method on an account with an active subscription — add a new one first.

**Supported cards:** Visa, Mastercard, and American Express are supported in most countries. Prepaid and virtual cards work as long as they allow international online payments.

**Security:** Card numbers are stored by our PCI-compliant payment processor, not by ConnectTel directly. We never see or store your full card number.

**Still having trouble?** If a card is rejected when adding it, the most common cause is that online or international payments are disabled on the card. Enable them in your bank app and try again.
