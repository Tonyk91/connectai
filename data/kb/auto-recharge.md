# Setting up and cancelling auto-recharge

Auto-recharge automatically adds credit to your ConnectTel balance when it drops below a threshold, so you never run out mid-call.

**Turn it on:**
1. Go to **Account → Auto-recharge**.
2. Choose a *trigger balance* (the level that triggers a recharge) and a *recharge amount*.
3. Select the payment method to use and save.

When your balance falls below the trigger, we charge your saved method for the recharge amount and add the credit immediately.

**Change the amounts:** You can adjust the trigger or amount at any time from the same screen. Changes apply to the next recharge.

**Turn it off:** Toggle auto-recharge to **Off**. No further automatic charges will be made. Any credit already on your balance stays available.

**Failed auto-recharge:** If the payment fails, we retry once after a few hours and notify you. If it fails again, auto-recharge pauses until you update your payment method.

**Still seeing recharges after turning it off?** Make sure you saved the change and that you do not have a separate *subscription* renewing, which is billed independently of auto-recharge.
